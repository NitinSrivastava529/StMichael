Desk of Principal

As I walk down the corridors of the school every day, I hear the excited minds of the most energetic kids that I have come across, I see them every day trying to find their calling, trying to become something, trying to find their way in this world. That makes me realize that we are all here to make a difference to their world to be. We value individualism, creativity and innovation and strive to nurture these traits in our students. Our motto is “Dream. Believe and Achieve.
” We aim to teach our students to follow their dreams. Our aim is not just to impart knowledge to the students, but also teach them that the passion, persistence and hard work are the core of success. We teach our students that honesty, self -reliance and confidence are the pillars of a personality.
I am always excited and look forward to working with our students, staff and parents to make St. Michael's School the best school . My commitment to our community is to lead the school with enthusiasm and passion to enable is to reach our goals. Fr. Ambrose .M
(Principal)
