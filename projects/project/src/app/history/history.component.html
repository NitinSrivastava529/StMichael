OUR HISTORY

St. Michael's School is a Minority institution co-educational English Medium School. This institution was established in the year 2012 by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Jhansi, to cater primarily to the education of all the communities irrespective of caste,

creed or religion. The school is owned, and managed by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Jhansi. Rt. Rev. Peter Parapullil the Bishop of Jhansi is the patron & the President of all our Educational Instituions.
